Welcome to Bellezza

Ciao a tutti!Welcome to Bellezza, the best hair salon that you will ever go to! Founded in 1900 in Vancouver by Italian hairstylist Massimo Angeletti, Bellezza has been giving its clients the best and most expensive celebrity looks that they can ever receive for over 100 years!After its success its Vancouver drawing over 1 million clients per year, Bellezza has opened branches in other parts of the world, including Paris, Milan, Tokyo, New York City and much more. Bellezza has been ranked as the number #1 fashion hair stylist studio for decades, and has always played a dominant role in the fashion industry, styling celebrities like Naomi Campbell, Cindy Crawford, Tyra Banks, Heidi Klum, Leonardo DiCaprio, Brad Pitt, Fabio and David Beckham. By the time you step out of this salon, you will step out looking like a whole new person!
