FAQ

"Bellezza" is the Italian word for beauty. Interestingly, the name of the salon was actually "Beauty", but in order to stand out from the crowd, and to partly keep in touch with his Italian ancestry, in honor of his parents who have been long supportive of his hair-styling career, the founder Massimo Angeletti wanted to name it "Bellezza". Since then, "Bellezza" has made a big name in fashion today.

All ages are welcome! We take care of clients of all ages.

We use products that have been used in the fashion industry for a very long time, such as TreSemme, L'Oreal, Clariol, Dove, Sassoon, Göt2b and much much more. As our hair salon is not just any ordinary hair salon, but a hair styling salon where the most famous celebrities, in particular Tyra Banks, Naomi Campbell, Drew Barrymore, Heidi Klum, Brad Pitt, Leonardo DiCaprio, Tom Cruise and much more, we are using the safest and most reliable hair products in order to give our clients the upmost beautiful look they will ever have when they step out of this salon. Please be warned that if you have had an allergic reaction or irritated skin with some of the products that we use, we will do our best to accomodate you as a client, though we cannot guarantee that we will find products perfectly suitable for your skin. By choosing to go with your appointment at Bellezza, you hereby understand that we are not legally liable for any allergic reactions should such an unforeseen circumstance occur, so if you suspect you may have such a reaction, please consider testing first before your appointment.

As a hair salon that has been established since the 1900s, we have been adapting all different hair styles to accomodate those who are following most recent trends. We provide not only the best haircuts modelled after the hottest celebrities in the biz, but also other styles, including colouring, curling, straightening, perming, extensions, and much more, as you shall see in our services page. Be sure to check them out!

We work with the abovementioned brands that we get our products from (TreSemme, Dove, Sassoon....etc.), as well as cosmetic brands for our clients who aim to have photoshoot sessions after their drastic makeovers. The cosmetic brands that we work with include Chanel, Dior, and Armani. Once you exit the salon, you will out looking like a top model!
